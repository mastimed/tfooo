Play Among Us Online

Multiplayer Social Deduction Play With Friends

Among Us is a social‑deduction party game where teamwork and suspicion collide. Crewmates try to complete tasks and identify the impostor(s). Impostors win by eliminating crewmates or creating chaos with sabotages.

How to Play

Each round starts with secret roles. If you’re a Crewmate, focus on tasks, share clear information, and use meetings to compare timelines. If you’re an Impostor, blend in, use sabotage to split groups, and avoid leaving evidence.

Core Concepts

  • Tasks: progress the team’s win condition.
  • Meetings: share info, vote, and remove suspects.
  • Sabotage: forces movement and creates opportunities.

Winning Tips

  • As Crewmate: move in pairs, call out unusual routes, and confirm tasks when possible.
  • As Impostor: avoid over‑talking; create believable alibis; use lights/doors to isolate.
  • During meetings: ask “where, when, who was nearby?” instead of accusing randomly.

FAQ

What are the basic roles?

Most versions include Crewmates and Impostors, and some modes add special roles with extra abilities.

Is this game better with friends?

Yes—voice chat and a consistent group make deductions more interesting, but public lobbies can be fun too.
