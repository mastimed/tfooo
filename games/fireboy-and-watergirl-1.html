Play Fireboy and Watergirl 1 Online

Puzzle Co‑op Platformer

Fireboy and Watergirl 1 is a cooperative puzzle‑platformer set in the Forest Temple. The rules are simple: Fireboy survives fire but not water; Watergirl survives water but not fire; and green acid hurts both. Success is all about timing, switches, and teamwork.

Controls (Classic Keyboard)

  • Watergirl: W / A / D (jump / left / right)
  • Fireboy: Arrow keys (↑ / ← / →)

How to Win Levels

  • Collect diamonds when safe—then prioritize reaching the exits.
  • Use levers, buttons, and platforms to open paths for the other character.
  • Stop and plan: many rooms are “puzzles first, platforming second”.

Teamwork Tips

  • Call out timing (“3…2…1…go”) for moving platforms and gates.
  • When solo, move one character into a safe spot before switching.
