Play Sonic the Hedgehog Online

Platformer Speed Classic

Sonic the Hedgehog is the iconic speed platformer built around momentum. Collect rings, avoid spikes and enemies, and use slopes to build speed. Mastering jump timing and rolling is the key to staying fast without crashing.

Core Moves

  • Move: run left/right to build momentum.
  • Jump: clear gaps and enemies; bouncing can keep flow.
  • Roll / Spin: protect yourself and keep speed on slopes.

Tips for Cleaner Runs

  • Keep at least a few rings—one mistake won’t end your run.
  • Slow down before blind drops; speed is powerful but punishes misreads.
  • Use slope physics: rolling downhill can set up long jumps.
