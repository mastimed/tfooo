Play Mini Putt Online

Sports Mini Golf Casual

Mini Putt is a mini‑golf browser classic with physics‑based shots and creative obstacles. The goal is simple: finish each hole in as few strokes as possible.

How to Play

  • Place/aim using the mouse or touch (depending on version).
  • Pull back to set power, then release to putt.
  • Use walls and angles—sometimes a bank shot is the safest route.

Tips

  • Prioritize control over maximum power—small adjustments win holes.
  • Learn obstacle timing (moving parts) before committing to a shot.
  • When unsure, aim for a safe landing zone rather than the cup.
